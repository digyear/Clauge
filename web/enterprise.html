Who needs to talk to us

Clauge is free for personal, hobby, academic, and other noncommercial use under the PolyForm Noncommercial license. If you or your organization is using Clauge in any commercial context, you need a license — reach out here.

This includes

  • Solo contractors, freelancers, and consultants using Clauge on paid work
  • Startups, agencies, and small businesses deploying Clauge to their team
  • Mid-size and enterprise organizations rolling Clauge out to developers
  • Anyone embedding Clauge in a product or service they offer to others

What you get

  • A commercial license sized to your organization (pricing scales — a 5-person team pays meaningfully less than a 500-person one)
  • Priority support and a direct line to the maintainer
  • Security review assistance for your compliance team
  • Optional private deployment of the sync backend on your own Cloudflare account
  • Volume licensing and SLA terms negotiated per agreement
